Parents can create tasks and rewards. Kids can complete tasks, earn points, build streaks, and request rewards when they have enough points.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

ThumbsUp Family Rewards is designed for family habit, task, reward, and progress tracking. This policy explains the data the app may store or request while you use it.

Information you provide

The app may store information you enter, such as child and parent profile names, avatar choices, tasks, habits, completion history, points, rewards, reward requests, achievements, behaviour notes, and app settings.

Local storage and iCloud

The app may store data on your device using Apple platform storage. Some builds may use Apple iCloud or CloudKit to support sync or family sharing. iCloud and CloudKit data are handled through Apple services and your Apple ID/iCloud account.

Optional permissions

The app may ask for camera or photo library access so you can personalize a profile image. It may ask for microphone or speech recognition access to help turn spoken input into habit or task ideas. These permissions are optional and controlled by iOS.

AI-assisted features

Some builds may include AI-assisted habit, note, or insight features. If remote AI features are enabled in a build, text you choose to process may be sent to the configured AI service. Do not enter sensitive information into AI-assisted fields unless you are comfortable processing that text with the configured service.

Children's privacy

ThumbsUp Family Rewards is intended for use by families under parent or guardian supervision. Parents or guardians should decide what profile names, task details, rewards, notes, and images are appropriate to enter.

Data sharing

The app does not sell personal information. Data may be shared only when required for app functionality, such as iCloud/CloudKit sync, optional Apple system services, or optional AI-assisted features in builds where those features are enabled.

Deleting data

You can delete app data by removing content inside the app where controls are provided, by deleting the app from your device, or by managing associated iCloud data through Apple account/iCloud settings when iCloud sync is used.

Changes to this policy

This page may be updated as ThumbsUp Family Rewards changes. The date above identifies the latest version of this policy.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, use the developer contact information listed in App Store Connect, TestFlight, or the App Store listing for ThumbsUp Family Rewards.
